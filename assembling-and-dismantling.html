Assembling and Dismantling (for Shifting Machines)

Shifting heavy machines sounds simple and straightforward- until you actually do the task. These machines most often cannot be moved as a single unit, given its complexity. This is why professional equipment dismantling and installation services become crucial.

At YM Plus, our expert team ensures the safe transport and reinstallation of machines without causing any damage, offering the best machine dismantling services across the UAE.

How We Perform The Task

No matter how complex the machinery, our machine relocation services in the UAE are carried out safely and accurately.

  • Assessment: We assess the machine conditions and other relocation requirements.

  • Planning: Once the requirements are sorted, we devise a systematic plan for the efficient dismantling and assembling of the machines.

  • Dismantling: The machines are carefully dismantled and grouped into transportable containers.

  • Reassembly and Installation: After reaching the site, the machines are carefully reassembled and installed.

  • Testing: A test is run after the installation to ensure the functionality of the machine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you handle, relocate and install heavy equipment?

Yes, our expert technicians efficiently handle heavy and high-value equipment, ensuring careful dismantling, safe shifting and reassembling. We are equipped with all the necessary tools and safe transportation support to carry out the process smoothly.

How do you ensure safety throughout the dismantling and assembling process?

Our technicians plan the process strategically, conduct risk assessments, securely pack the dismantled parts, transport with caution and reassemble the equipment safely. We also follow proper safety practices, including the use of PPE kits.
