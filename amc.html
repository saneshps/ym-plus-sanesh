What is an AMC?

Annual maintenance service contract is a service agreement between the client and the provider( manufacturer or a maintenance company) that outlines the terms, scopes, maintenance cost and other support services that ensures the unhindered performance of machines.

Our industrial equipment/ machinery AMC services are designed to meet our client’s needs, offering comprehensive maintenance solutions that ensure your machines operate smoothly, minimize breakdowns and maintain consistent performance.

Our AMC Services

From regular inspections to preventive servicing, our AMCs offer prompt repairs, replacements and technical support. Our major services include:

  • Preventive maintenance (to mitigate unexpected halt and breakdowns)

  • Repairs and trouble shooting

  • Replacement of certain parts (which depends on the contract type)

  • Emergency support or on-call service.

Types of AMC

AMCs are offered in two main types:

  • Comprehensive AMC

  • Non-Comprehensive AMC

Comprehensive AMC

Just as the name suggests, Comprehensive AMC offers complete coverage from labour and spare parts to repair. It typically includes:

  • Regular maintenance

  • Repairs and servicing

  • Replacement of damaged parts

  • Labour charges

  • Emergency support

Non-Comprehensive AMC

Non-comprehensive AMCs on the other hand only service/ labour. It focuses on preventive maintenance and check-ups and doesn't include the cost of replacement parts.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is comprehensive AMC different from non-comprehensive AMC?

Comprehensive AMC covers both maintenance services and spare parts, including major breakdown repair. Non-comprehensive AMC on the other hand, covers only routine inspection and labour charges. Spare parts and major repairs are charged separately.

What are the exclusions in an AMC?

AMCs usually don’t cover damage due to misuse,third-party repairs and consumables.

What is the standard duration of AMC?

The standard duration of AMC is one year, although some contracts may include the option for multi-year.

What is the typical cost of AMC?

The cost of an AMC depends on several factors such as the type of machine, frequency of usage, the level of coverage required and several other factors.
